Meet Our Team

Mr. HR Keshava Murthy: Founder

Our journey into the Construction Industry started in Bangalore in 2007, by its Founder Mr. HR Keshava Murthy, under the banner Gift to Nature Constructions. We have grown to become one of the Bangalore’s leading construction contractors.

“Success to a builder is not measured by financial performance. A builder becomes successful when his client gives him repeat business and when most of his business comes from referrals. This is a real achievement to a builder.”

Rashmi HK: Managing Partner

The legacy of passion to build innovative Eco Friendly Homes passed on from Mr. HR Keshava Murthy to his daughter Ms HK Rashmi who continued with the same experts and professionals under the banner Krithi Concepts. The founding members still own and run the business on family values of honesty, reliability and fairness as an integral part to the way of work.

“I remember my Father telling me ‘Do not sell houses; sell homes’. For me it’s all about teamwork. Teamwork not only with my staff, but teamwork with my clients, hand holding them to understand their requirements at every level. I want our teams to enjoy their work and I want our clients to enjoy working with us. Whether it’s a renovation of a building or a high rise office block, we are committed to building high quality facilities with no compromise on workmanship.”
